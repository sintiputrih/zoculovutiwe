Kylie Jenner Steps Out in L.A. in a Fuzzy LaQuan Smith Dress

Kylie Jenner wore a high-fashion take on the cozy trend.

Over the weekend, the Kylie Cosmetics founder was photographed stepping out in her second LaQuan Smith-designed look of the week, dressed in a white textured-knit minidress with a coordinating oversized coat. Jenner also wore bright-white peep-toe pumps by Amina Muaddi and carried the crystal embellished Bon Bon top handle bag from Jimmy Choo. She further accessorized her look with dainty silver hoop earrings and a matching white pedicure, and she had her hair slicked back into a chic bun.

Earlier this week, Jenner wore Smith again, that time opting for a sexy maroon mesh catsuit paired with a leather trench coat.

BACKGRID

The beauty mogul also took to Instagram to document her winter-inspired number. "Ok I'm ready for warm weather now," Jenner captioned the snapshot, referencing what seemed to be a chillier night in Los Angeles.

As Jenner opted for a glamorous approach to her wardrobe for Saturday, she switched into more casual attire for a hike with her three-year-old daughter, Stormi Webster, on Sunday. For the mother-daughter date, Jenner wore a brown cropped tank and matching biker shorts by SET ACTIVE with white tube socks and chunky Nike platform sneakers. As for beauty, she pulled her hair back into a casual ponytail.

In her Instagram Stories, Jenner shared clips of herself going for a jog with a friend and of her daughter playing with her dolls outside as they soaked up the L.A. sunshine together.
