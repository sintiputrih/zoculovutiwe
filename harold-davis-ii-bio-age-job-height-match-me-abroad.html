Meet Match Me Abroad's Harold Davis II! His Age, Job, Height

TLC’s new dating series, Match Me Abroad, premiered on 14 May 2023 and introduced to viewers seven singles hoping to find love outside of America. Following their unlucky history within their home country, these seven singles are searching for love outside of the United States and Harold Davis II is one of them.

In this writing now, let us tell you who Harold is. Our first perception of him is that he is autistic and struggling with knowing what women want. That is why he thought of seeking expert help to find himself a partner.

Keep reading to know more.

Harold Davis II On TLC’s Match Me Abroad

Harold Davis II and other singles on TLC‘s Match Me Abroad are split among three matchmakers South America, Europe, and Africa. As part of this, Harold also flies to the Czech Republic to find his life partner under Prague-based matchmaker Katarina Nemcova.

With an 80 percent success rate, Katarina flatters herself in uniting singles. Long before taking on Harold and Michelle as clients on Match Me Abroad, she launched her matchmaking service, Find L’Amour, in 2014. The company has appeared in Forbes Slovakia among other famed portals. Oh yes, and she is likened to Sima Taparia of Indian Matchmaking!

Harold, on Match Me Abroad is introduced as someone who lacks confidence in his social interactions. “I love you, and if you love me, that would be great,” he is seen saying to one woman, before pulling out an engagement ring. To the cameras, he also shares that he is hoping to find a Czech woman to fall in love with and share his rock collection with.

Fortunately, for Harold, Match Me Abroad is most likely not scripted. Katarina, like other matchmakers on the show, is believed to have for real taken up the task of giving her clients a chance at love.

Is Harold Davis II Dating Anyone?

Harold Davis II’s relationship status was unclear as of May 2023. Recently, even his brother admitted that he does not know if or not Harold gets matched with someone permanently.

So, at the time of this writing, it was not known if Harold found the one he was looking for, someone blunt and honest.

Harold Davis II Job

Harold has been a full-time radiation technician/research technician at Triad in Northern New Mexico since January 2022.

Per LinkedIn, he has continued working as a part-time designer at createtheleader.com since January 2009. He also has not stopped drawing for his merch line alongside his day job.

Harold is a BFA in Drawing, Art History graduate (1999 – 2007) from the University Of Texas at San Antonio. While there, he also was the founding president of Clay Bodies, a ceramic clay club.

So, Harold was a “starving artist” for the longest time before starting a new career as a radiation technician.

Previously he also worked full-time at BHI Energy, Walmart, and as a head evening receptionist at Blessed Sacrament Catholic Church.

Harold among other things also makes money by selling his merchandise on the spreadshirt.com shop. For instance, his “Unisex Stars & Stripes T-Shirt” is put on sale at $32.99. He says his T-shirt designs are from his art, interest, and a few images from friends that have the desire to create but lack the drive.
