About Her Parents: Katherine Kady Allen, Tim Ellen’s Daughter

Tim and Laura Deibel, his former spouse, had only one daughter, Kady, who was born in December 1989.

Her parents were longtime friends from high school who tied the knot on April 7, 1984, following a protracted courtship. They brought Kady into the world a few years later.

Laura Divorced: Who Was Granted Custody Over Kady?

However, in 1999, following more than ten years of marriage, they formally split. Laura then filed for divorce, claiming that they could not reconcile.

The divorce of Katherine’s parents was completed in 2003. Tim and Laura decided to resolve their disagreements without going to trial.

Furthermore, until their daughter reached adulthood, they both had joint custody of her.

Tim Was Gone For The Most Of Kady’s Early Years

Tim’s married life was in disarray during the beginning of the 1990s. His career took off when the smash television program Home Improvement became widely popular.

Tim’s hectic work caused him to become estranged from his spouse and kids. He didn’t realize his marriage was ending since he was so focused on his work. “How many times do I have to tell myself that on my deathbed I’m not gonna say, ‘Gee, I wish I’d spent more time at the office?'” he even said to USA Weekend in 1997.

Furthermore, Tim acknowledged that he was always focused on advancing his job and never truly gave his family any thought. In addition, he said “I let my family slide” in favor of his work.

Furthermore, he attributed the chaos to his job schedule. Even more than that, Allen missed seeing his daughter’s upbringing—something that any loving father would be sad to miss. He stated, “I never see my daughter at all,” to The Detroit News.

However, Kady’s mother Laura was also by her father’s side. The new graduate of Western Michigan used to perform for pennies on the dollar at Kutlenios’s club in 1982. She was making good money and supporting her spouse at the time.

Allen, Katherine Kady ties with the father, the stepmother, and the half-sibling

Kady’s father remarried just three years after the couple’s divorce was finalized. On October 7, 2006, he was married to the actress Jane Hajduk, his lover.

In addition, the couple began dating in 2001. The advent of their first kid added even more brightness to their already beautiful lives. On March 28, 2009, Tim welcomed Elizabeth Allen as his second daughter.

No matter how her early years transpired, Kady continues to have a great deal of regard for her father. She also gets along well with Elizabeth, her half-sister and stepmother.

She has even accompanied all three of them to many red-carpet premieres. It’s obvious from that that she gets along well with her stepfamily.

Tim Desires To Change As A Father To Better Serve His Daughters

During an open interview with Parade Magazine, Tim went to considerable measures to acknowledge his shortcomings as a father. However, he didn’t cry himself to death as is anticipated to happen.

He made the decision to spend more time with his second daughter once she was born. Although Tim wasn’t an approved parent to his oldest daughter, the Last Man Standing actor has undergone some notable transformations.

“I was gone so much in my first marriage, I love the moments when I engage with my youngest daughter now,” he said to the magazine. Even if having a tea party on the ground is not my thing, I’ll still do it because it’s an experience that I will never forget.
