BofA treats cardholders to free museum admission WSOC TV

CHARLOTTE, N.C. — Bank of America customers can receive free admission to several museums in Charlotte and others across the Carolinas the first full weekend of every month, as part of the bank’s “Museum on Us” program.

In Charlotte, Bank of America, Merrill or Bank of America Private Bank (U.S. Trust) cardholders get one free general admission to Bechtler Museum of Modern Art, Harvey B. Gantt Center for African-American Arts + Culture, Mint Museum Randolph and Mint Museum Uptown.

Other participating attractions in the Carolinas include the Asheville Museum of Science, Marbles Kids Museum in Raleigh, Greensboro Children’s Museum, Reynolda House Museum of American Art in Winston-Salem, Columbia Museum of Art, Gibbes Museum of Art in Charleston and The Children’s Museum of the Upstate in Greenville, South Carolina.

Be sure to bring a photo ID and your Bank of America, Merrill or U.S. Trust credit or debit card to receive your free entry.

Upcoming dates:

Jan. 7-8

Feb. 4-5

March 4-5

April 1-2

Museum hours may vary. Check with individual venues for more information.
