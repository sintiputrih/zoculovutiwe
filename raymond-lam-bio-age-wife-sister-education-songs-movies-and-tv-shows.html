Who is Raymond Lam? Raymond Lam Biography and Wiki

Raymond Lam is a Chinese actor and singer who was born in Xiamen and brought up in Hong Kong, China. He is well known as the King of Chok after playing his role in the Mysteries of Love. His other notable work is seen in television dramas such as A Step into the Past, Moonlight Resonance, Line Walker, Highs and Lows among others you will find listed as you read on.

Lam and his family moved to Hong Kong when he was only two years old. He was raised there alongside his sister and he had a dream of becoming a dancer as a child. Besides Chinese, the singer has Spanish ancestry from his paternal great grand mother. He speaks fluently in English, Mandarin, Cantonese, and Hokkien.

Raymond Lam Education

The Mysteries of Love star is an alumnus of Kiangsu and Chekiang Mandarin-speaking primary school located in pre-Handover, Hong Kong. He then joined St. Paul’s College in Hong Kong for his Secondary studies. Afterward, Lam attended Xiamen University for 1st Year, and relocated to the United States of America where he completed his degree courses in architecture and music from the University of Southern California.

Raymond Lam TVB

In September 1998, Lam graduated from the 13th TVB artists training class where he was an auditing student. He was then offered work as a walk-on in several television series and as a host for TVB8. After 5 years with the television network, Lam received the TVB Anniversary Award for Most Improved Male Artiste in 2003.

Later on in 2018, Lam left TVB and signed with Sky High Entertainment owned by Louis Koo. Nevertheless, he made his come back to TVB during the press conference of the network’s Homecoming on 17th March 2019. Besides TVB, Lam also works with The Voice Entertainment group.
