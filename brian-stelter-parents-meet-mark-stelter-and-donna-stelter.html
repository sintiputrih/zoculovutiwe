Brian Stelter Parents: Meet Mark Stelter And Donna Stelter

Mark Stelter and Donna Stelter are the parents of Brian Stelter.

Although Brian’s parents’ names have been identified, their details are under the radar.

Brian Stelter is an American former TV news anchor.

Born September 3, 1985, in Damascus, Maryland, Brian Stelter attended Damascus High School, graduating in 2003. He then obtained a bachelor’s degree in Mass Communication from Towson University in 2007.

While at Towson University, he served as editor-in-chief of The Towerlight from 2005 to 2007.

Prior to joining CNN as a chief media correspondent and host of the CNN program Reliable Sources, Stelter worked as a media reporter for The New York Times and editor of TVNewser.

In addition to serving as an on-air personality, he also served as a media writer for CNN.com.

In 2013, he published the New York Times best-selling book, “Top of the Morning” where he examined the television rivalry between the Today Show and Good Morning America.

On August 18, 2022, CNN canceled Reliable Sources with Brian Stelter. Stelter is set to depart the network after its final episode on August 21.
