The Kardashian Sisters Look Lean and Mean in Black in Their New Series of Selfies

Have mirror, will selfie. The Kardashian-Jenner sisters were reportedly at the listening party for Kanye West's new album, Swish this weekend when they came across social media gold: the perfect bathroom for selfies. Kourtney and Kim Kardashian were the first to test out the mirror, and took a photo of themselves in the neon light.

"Studio hoes. #SWISH" Kourtney captioned a photo on Instagram of the two of them wearing all black and flaunting their signature curves.

Youngest sister Kylie Jenner (who is also wearing all black) joined in on the photo session for a group photo that Kourtney simply titled "#SWISH"

Although Kendall Jenner didn't get the bathroom memo, Kim shared a close-up selfie of the two of them together, smiling coyly with their heads together and the caption "Keep Up"
