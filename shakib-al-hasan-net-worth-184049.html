Shakib Al Hasan Net Worth

Shakib Al Hasan is a Bangladeshi cricket all-rounder who was born on March 24, 1987 in Magura District, Bangladesh. He made his debut in 2007 and quickly rose to become the world's top-ranked Test all-rounder in 2011. In 2015, he made history by becoming the first all-rounder to be ranked number one in Tests, ODIs, and T20s simultaneously.
