Floccesy Ranch (Japanese: サンギ牧場 Sangi Ranch) is a farm-like location in southwest Unova. It is located north of Route 20.

Description

Floccesy Ranch is visited near the beginning of Pokémon Black 2 and White 2. It is a small farm adjacent to a river. According to the owner, it started when a fence was made to protect Pokémon. Nowadays, many different species of wild Pokémon, including several not originally native to Unova, can be found. The player can interact with tame Mareep, which roam free. A small forest is located behind the ranch. Just like Floccesy Town and Route 20, it can rain here, all raining at the same time.

The player must travel here to issue the spare Town Map they received in Aspertia City to their rival, Hugh. After the two participate in a Pokémon battle, the owners of the ranch enlist them to find their lost Herdier. Hugh is very worried, constantly mentioning that "...your Pokémon might be gone forever!", prompting the ranch owner to wonder if something had happened to him. After going through the "forest", the missing Pokémon is found being cornered by a member of Team Plasma. Upon noticing the player, he will angrily yell at the player, then mention he is a member of Team Plasma. If the player says they have never heard of it, he will give a quick summary of what happened two years before. Then he will throw TM21 (Frustration) at the player, using this as an opportunity to flee.

The ranch owner's wife will heal the player's Pokémon if talked to.

A Hidden Grotto is located north of the farm, on the east side of the river. Surf is required to reach it.

In the anime

Floccesy Ranch in the anime

Floccesy Ranch appeared in The Light of Floccesy Ranch!. Like in the games, a herd of tame Mareep were seen around it. The Ranch is supervised by Ellie and her grandfather, Travon. Ellie is responsible for keeping the herd of Mareep in check and within the Ranch limits with the help of her Ampharos.

Unfortunately, Ampharos lacked self-confidence to carry out its task, causing the Mareep to constantly disregard it and get away. However, thanks to some support from Ash and his friends, as well as a confrontation with Team Rocket as they tried to steal the Mareep, Ampharos was able to master its Thunder Punch and gained enough confidence to not let the Mareep wander away under its watch.

Trivia

  • This location's music, サンギ牧場 Floccesy Ranch, is an arrangement of 視線！やまおとこ Trainers' Eyes Meet (Hiker).
  • In the first patch of grass by the entrance, there is one tile that appears to be grass, but is instead a normal tile. The bottom of the player's sprite is not covered by the grass when standing on it, and it does not make a noise when the player steps on it. Wild Pokémon are not able to encountered on this tile, even with the use of Sweet Scent or Honey.
