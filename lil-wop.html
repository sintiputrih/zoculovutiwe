Lil Wop- Biography

Lil Wop (also known as Lil Wop 17 and Little Wopster) is a young American rapper best known for his smash tune “Lost My Mind,” which was released in July 2017. Furthermore, he is well-known for his other tracks such as “Broadway,” “BackWoods,” and “Safe House,” as well as being Gucci Mane’s ‘protégé’ and signing a record deal with Mane’s 1017 Eskimo Label.

Lil Wop- Professional Career

Lil Wop debuted in the music industry in 2014, at the age of 19, when he began posting mixtapes and tracks to the famous music streaming service SoundCloud. Singles such as “Perky’s & Molly’s,” “Hyped Out,” and “The Wopster” were among his early works (hence one of his stage names).

In the years thereafter, he has collaborated with his sister and promising young rapper Trippie Redd, with whom he has recorded three more songs: “Beast Mode,” “Awakening My Inner Beast,” and “Rock the World Trippie.”

Lil Wop signed with Bases Loaded Records after releasing “Wopster The Mixtape” in December 2016. His debut studio-recorded mixtape, “Wopavelli,” hit the charts in March 2017, and barely a month later, he released another mixtape, “Wake N Bake,” hosted by DJ Holiday, which spawned multiple popular tracks, including “Gotcha Bitch” and “BackWoods.”

With his reputation on the rise, Lil Wop released “Wopavelli 2,” his second major and, at the time, biggest mixtape, in June 2017. It included ManMan Savage and Red Smash as special guests and spawned smash hits such as “Safe House,” “Dead People,” “Broadway,” and “Lost My Mind.” Lil Wop ‘caught an ear’ of his idol and one of the finest rappers in modern hip hop – Gucci Mane – and became his protégé, signing a record deal with 1017 Eskimo Label, as the latter earned a tremendous dosage of good evaluations from the audience,’ scoring’ nearly 2.8 million views on YouTube.

In October 2017, Lil Wop reunited with Trippie Redd for the second time, and the duo produced a collaboration EP named “Angels & Demons.” Later the same month, his third mixtape, “Wopavelli 3,” was released, with 17 songs, including the singles “Workstar,” “Friday the 13th,” and “Paid In Full,” which included Gucci Mane as a guest star. Lil Wop was chosen the “2017 Trapper of the Year” for all of his efforts and because of the massive amount of recognition he gained virtually overnight.

In August 2018, Lil Wop released “Silent Hill,” a horror-movie-inspired 10-track EP that spawned smash hits such as “The Ring,” “Hills Has Eyes,” and “Bride of Chucky,” with JDA Mook and Big Adolf as guest stars. After a two-month delay, he released his fourth Wopavelli series mixtape, simply titled “Wopavelli 4” in October 2018 — the 19 songs featured some previously released hit singles such as “Real Or Fake”, “Ghoul”, and “Hugh Hefner”. His most recent single, “The Slime,” was released in March 2019.
