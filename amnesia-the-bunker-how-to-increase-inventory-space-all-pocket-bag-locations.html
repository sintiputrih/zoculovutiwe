Amnesia: The Bunker How to Increase Inventory Space [All Pocket Bag Locations]

The bunker is filled with loose loot like fuel, cloth, and various key items. However, players have limited inventory space when starting out and they won’t be able to carry much.

To increase your inventory in Amnesia The Bunker, you will need to collect Pocket Bags found around the map. There are seven pocket pags available in each playthrough but there are 12 possible spawn locations.

In this guide, I will explain how to increase inventory space and showcase all Pocket Bag spawn locations in the game.

If you’re interested in key item guides, check out our article on how to find the Wrench in Amnesia The Bunker.

How to Increase Inventory Space in Amnesia The Bunker

To increase your inventory space in Amnesia The Bunker and carry more items, you’ll need to find Pocket Bags. These are small satchels that can be found around the map and are automatically equipped on pickup.

Each bag will increase your inventory space slightly and you can collect up to seven of these bags to reach the maximum inventory space.

However, there are 12 possible spawn locations for these bags, so you’ll probably need to check them all if you want to find all seven bags.

Wrapping up

To sum up, players can increase their inventory space in Amnesia The Bunker by finding Pocket Bags. There are 12 potential spawn locations for Pocket Bags and 7 of them will be chosen randomly in every game to have a Pocket Bag.

Check all the locations mentioned above until you find seven pocket bags and get the achievement Pack Rat.
