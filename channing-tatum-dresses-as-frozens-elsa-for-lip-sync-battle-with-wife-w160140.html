Channing Tatum Dresses as Frozen's Elsa for Lip Sync Battle With Wife

From Magic Mike to. . . a Frozen princess?! Channing Tatum got dolled up as Frozen’s Elsa for an upcoming Lip Sync Battle against his wife, Jenna Dewan Tatum.

The 22 Jump Street actor, 35, who will most likely belt out “Let It Go,” frolics across the dance floor in Elsa’s sparkly blue dress and wears a white wig during the hilarious performance. (The 2013 Disney character was voiced by Idina Menzel.)

“I just really hope that my marriage isn’t over after tonight,” he jokes in a sneak peek.

The Supergirl actress, 35, quips: “Our house is divided at the moment. We’re going to war!”

As previously reported, the Gambit hunk also twerks to a Beyonce hit. Queen Bey is rumored to be making a surprise appearance during the episode.
