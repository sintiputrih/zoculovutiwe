Mikhail Mishustin Biography / Wiki

Mikhail Mishustin is a Russian economist and politician serving as Prime Minister of Russia since 16 January 2020. Mikhail previously served as Director of the Federal Tax Service from 2010 to 2020.

On 15 January 2020, Mishustin was nominated for Prime Minister of the Russian Federation by President Vladimir Putin following the resignation of Dmitry Medvedev. Hearings on his appointment were held in the State Duma on 16 January, and he was confirmed to the office that day

Mikhail Mishustin Career

He first began working as a director of a test laboratory facility. Mishustin began working at the International Computer Club (ICC), where he worked on facilitating the integration of Russian and Western information technologies since 1992. He ultimately headed the board of the International Computer Club. Mishustin joined the state service as an assistant for information systems for accounting and control over the receipt of tax payments to the head of the State tax service of the Russian Federation.
