Angie Ballard: The Paralympic Champion and the Reality TV Star

Angie Ballard’s name may be recognized by followers of sports and entertainment. She is a former Australian wheelchair racer who competed in the Paralympics, winning four medals, including two golds.

Early Life and Accident

Despite the obstacles, Angie never gave up on her aspirations. She acclimated to her new surroundings and learned how to use a wheelchair. She also finished her schooling, graduating from high school. She developed a good attitude and determination to overcome her impairment.

Paralympic Career
