Kim Kardashian Poses as First Lady

Kim Kardashian West has stripped down naked, been covered in paint, and posed with a glass of champagne balanced on her faux derrière for photoshoots, but nothing can quite compare to her latest magazine cover:

The reality star poses as "America's New First Lady" on the cover of Interview Magazine's newest issue, photographed by Steven Klein. In the shot, where she sits beside her daughter North West, Kardashain appears to channel Jackie Kennedy with a '60s hairstyle, pearl earrings, a white mod skirt suit, white gloves and a kisslock purse.
