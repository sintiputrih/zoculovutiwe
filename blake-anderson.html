Blake Anderson

Anderson in 2015, photo by prphotos.com

Birth Name: Blake Raymond Anderson

Place of Birth: Sacramento, California, United States

Date of Birth: March 2, 1984

Ethnicity:
*mother – German, one quarter Bohemian Czech

Blake Anderson is an American actor, comedian, producer, screenwriter, and fashion designer. He is a member of comedy group Mail Order Comedy, along with Adam DeVine, Anders Holm, and Kyle Newacheck. The group created fictional rap band The Wizards and created, and starred on, the show Workaholics. He also had a voice role on the show Voltron: Legendary Defender.

His mother is of three quarters German and one quarter Bohemian Czech descent. He was raised in Concord, California. Blake has a daughter with his former wife Rachael Finley.

Blake’s maternal grandfather was James Frank Blazek (the son of Frank James Blazek and Elizabeth Marie Osterberger). Blake’s grandfather James was born in Iowa. Blake’s great-grandfather Frank was the son of Charles Joseph Blazek and Anna Vratrsovsky, who were born in Iowa, both of them to Bohemian/Czech parents. Elizabeth was the daughter of German parents, John Osterberger and Katharine/Katie Dorna.

Blake’s maternal grandmother was Mary Anne Heil (the daughter of William Charles Heil and Elizabeth Ann Kauffman). Blake’s grandmother Mary Anne was born in Iowa, and was of German descent, with roots in Baden-Württemberg. William was the son of Joseph Tobias Heil and Catherine Elisabeth Acks. Elizabeth was the daughter of Albert Anthony Kaufmann and Francisca Agnes Kaercher.
