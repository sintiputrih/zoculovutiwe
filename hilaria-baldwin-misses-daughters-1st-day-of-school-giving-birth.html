Hilaria Baldwin Misses Daughter's 1st Day of School Giving Birth

Busy day! Hilaria Baldwin was in the hospital delivering baby No. 5 when her daughter, Carmen, started her 2020 school year.

“Another semester of distance learning,” the Living Clearly Method author, 36, captioned a Wednesday, September 9, Instagram photo of her eldest child. “I’m sorry to miss your first day, my Carmen Gabriela. Thank you @violetgaynor for stepping in and making it special while I am away at the hospital with the new baby.”

The “Mom Brain” podcast cohost concluded, “Love that she needs her handbag for going to school at home,” referencing the pink purse Carmen held in the social media upload. The little one also displayed a sign marking her first day of second grade.

The fitness guru and her husband, Alec Baldwin, also share Rafael, 5, Leonardo, 3, and Romeo, 2. They announced their fifth child’s arrival on Wednesday.

“We had a baby last night,” Hilaria captioned her Instagram reveal at the time. “He is perfect and we couldn’t be happier. Stay tuned for a name.”

The actor, 62, who is also the father of daughter Ireland Baldwin, 24, with his ex-wife, Kim Basinger, added with a post of his own: “Número Cinco está aqui. (Para mi, número seis), [translating to, No. 5 is here. For me, No. 6]. I love you, @hilariabaldwin. My karma is your karma. Your karma is mine.”
