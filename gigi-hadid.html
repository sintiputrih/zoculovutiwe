Table of Biography

Gigi Hadid is an American model who has also starred in TV shows, movies, and music videos of various artists. Gigi Hadid has made 35 appearances on international “Vogue” magazine covers over four years.

Early Life and Childhood

Gigi Hadid’s full name is Jelena Noura Hadid and she was born on 23 April 1995. She is currently 28 years old and her birthplace is Los Angeles, California, the U.S. Her parents are Mohamed Hadid and Yolanda Hadid (née Van den Herik). Her father is a real estate developer and her mother is a former model. Her mother is Dutch whereas her father is Palestinian.

Talking about her ethnic background, she claims descent from Daher Al Omer, Prince of Nazareth, and the Sheik of Galilee from her father’s side. She also has two younger siblings namely Bella and Anwar. Both her siblings are also models. Moreover, she also has two older paternal half-sisters namely Marielle and Alana.

Caption: Gigi Hadid’s childhood photo with her later grandmother. Source: Instagram

Her former stepfather is David Foster. and he was married to Linda Thompson. Linda was once married to Caitlyn Jenner (aka Bruce Jenner). Technically, she is related to her best friends Kendall Jenner and Brody Jenner through marriage.

Moving on to her educational background, she graduated from Malibu High School in 2013. There, she was captain of the varsity volleyball team as well as a competitive horseback rider. Following high school, she moved to New York City to focus on her studies and her modeling career.

She then studied criminal psychology at The New School beginning in the fall of 2013. However, she suspended her studies and instead focused on her modeling career. Additionally, she revealed in 2014 that she was diagnosed with Hashimoto’s disease, and reiterated this in 2016. It is an autoimmune disease that gradually destroys the thyroid gland.

Moreover, sheGigi is also a big fan of the Power Rangers franchise and campaigned for the role of Kimberly Hart/Pink Ranger in Power Rangers in 2017.

Gigi Hadid – Relationship Status

Gigi Hadid has been in a romantic relationship with Zayn Malik since November 2015. The lovers broke up in March 2018 and reconciled in June 2018. Gigi Hadid – Professional Life

Gigi Hadid is a fashion model and an actress. She began her modeling career at 2 years old. Paul Marciano, of Guess, discovered her and hence she started with Baby Guess. However, she stopped concentrating in school and eventually got back, continuing to work with Marciano after completing school.

Moreover, she made her acting debut in 201 with the short film Virgin Eyes as the character Andrea. She is also most popular as a “Sports Illustrated” model and her recurring role on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills from 2012 to 2016. Then in November 2014, she made her debut in the Top 50 Models ranking at Models.com. Also, in 2016, the British Fashion Council named her the International Model of the Year.

Additionally, she has also shot three campaigns with Guess as an adult. After moving to New York City and signing with IMG Models in 2013, she made her New York Fashion Week debut in February 2014. There, she walked for Desigual’s show. In the same month. she also got her break in high fashion on the cover of CR Fashion Book magazine.

Similarly, on 15 July 2014, she starred alongside actor and model Patrick Schwarzenegger in fashion designer Tom Ford’s Eyewear autumn/winter campaign. She then co-hosted the Daily Front Row’s Fashion Media Awards event held in New York City on 5 September 2014.

She also starred in campaigns for Tom Ford F/W 2014, Tom Ford Velvet Orchard Fragrance, and Tom Ford Beauty 2014. Further, she appeared on the cover of Galore magazine and Carine Roitfeld’s CR Fashion Book in 2014. Moreover, she and her sister Bella started the Spring/Summer campaign season together. They starred alongside one another in both that season’s Fendi and Moschino campaigns.

After 2015

She then starred as the face of the S/S 2017 campaign for Max Mara accessories, Stuart Weitzman, and DSQUARED2. They presented the second Tommy Hilfiger and her ready-to-wear collection in February 2017 for the Spring season.

Moreover, she was the photographer of the Versus (Versace) Spring Summer 2017 campaign featuring singer Zayn Malik and model Adwoah Aboah. As a photographer, she also photographed a special summer edition of V Magazine titled She Journal featuring Polaroids of fashion industry colleagues, celebrities, and close friends.

She has been featured on four March 2017 Vogue covers in the United States, Britain, China, and the inaugural Arabia edition. She also appeared on the covers for the CR Fashion Book (Spring/Summer 2017), Jolie (April 2017), and The Daily (Spring 2017). She was then featured on the May 2017 covers for the Netherlands editions of Vogue, Cosmopolitan, and Glamour. She appeared on the June/July 2017 cover of US Harper’s Bazaar too.

Additionally, she has also starred in editorials for Vogue US in April 201 and for LOVE Magazine in Spring/Summer 2017. During Fall/Winter 2017 Fashion Month in New York, Milan, and Paris, she opened the shows for Jeremy Scott, Anna Sui, Versus (Versace), Alberta Ferretti, Missoni, H&M, and Balmain. She also closed the shows for Isabel Marant, Moschino, Max Mara, and Anna Sui in these events.

The Daily Front Row’s third annual Fashion Los Angeles Awards honored her for the Best Design Debut for her collection with Tommy Hilfiger. In addition to this, she received the title of the face of Guess in 2012.
