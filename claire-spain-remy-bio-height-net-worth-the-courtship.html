Claire Spain-Rémy Job

According to Claire Spain-Remy’s LinkedIn, she works as a Healthcare Consultant. Claire graduated from Duke University Medical School in 1985. She completed her OB-GYN residency at Madigan Army Medical Center in Tacoma WA in 1989. In 2010, she earned Masters Medical Management from the University of Southern California.

After a tour of duty in Seoul, Korea, Claire served on the faculty of Madigan’s OB-Gyn Residency program. After leaving the military she served as a member of the OB-GYN faculty of Tacoma Family Medicine’s residency program and OB Access Fellowship for Family Practice physicians.

Furthermore, according to Doximity, Claire earned her medical license in WA State in 1987 that will expire in 2024. She has been professionally active for over 37 years. Furthermore, she also earned professional memberships with the American Association for Physician Leadership – AAPL and the American College of Obstetricians and Gynecologists.

Moreover, Claire affiliates with MultiCare Tacoma General Hospital, MultiCare Good Samaritan Hospital, MultiCare Allenmore Hospital, and MultiCare Auburn Medical Center.

Claire ended her administrative career after serving as the Chief Physician Officer for MultiCare Health System where she had clinical and operational responsibility for almost 800 employed physicians, nurse practitioners, and physician’s assistants, more than 100 ambulatory and hospital-based physician practices, and the MultiCare Research Institute.

More about her career, she spent 12 years in private practice in Tacoma, WA, followed by the role of Specialty Medical Director for MultiCare Health system, then a 5 hospital healthcare system in the South Puget Sound of Washington. She has served as a healthcare consultant with ValueIQ. She serves on the boards of PacificSource Health Plans, the YMCA of Pierce and Kitsap Counties, the Tacoma/Pierce County chapter of Habitat for Humanity and is a member of the Duke Medical Alumni Council.
