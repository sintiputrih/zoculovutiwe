Zelda: Breath of the Wild guide: Hyrule Castle maps

You can wind you way through Hyrule Castle’s serpentine paths in any way you like. This makes exploring the castle somewhat confusing. Also, so does its layout. That's why we created maps to show you where everything is — rooms, doors, shortcuts and notable items. Use these to get your bearings.

You'll notice that there are two maps: one for Hyrule Castle’s exterior and one for Hyrule Castle’s interior. The exterior map shows you where each door into Hyrule Castle takes you. (The doors are all pink on the map.) The interior map shows you where the rooms are.
