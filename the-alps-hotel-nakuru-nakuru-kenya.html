The Alps Hotel Nakuru, Nakuru, Kenya

THE MEDIEVAL BARLocated at the rooftop, the Medieval bar provides a captivating view of the lovely stretch of the Mau Hills, Hyrax prehistoric hills and the expansive lake Nakuru.
The bar stocks a wide variety of drinks; both local and international origins, thus its fame for the Alp high end services by our professional mixologist.
This is a perfect place to unwind with soothing background music, premium selection of world class aged wine, whisky, cocktails & barbeque.
