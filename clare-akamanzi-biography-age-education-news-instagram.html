Clare Akamanzi Biography; Age, Education, News, Instagram

Clare Akamanzi is a Rwandan lawyer, public administrator, businesswoman and politician

From February 2017 to September 2023, she was the executive director and chief executive officer of the Rwanda Development Board. The position is a cabinet-level appointment made by Rwanda’s President.

Clare Akamanzi Age

How old is Clare Akamanzi? Clare Akamanzi is between the ages of 43–44

Clare Akamanzi Education

Where was Clare Akamanzi educated? Clare Akamanzi  has a Bachelor of Laws degree from Makerere University in Kampala, Uganda’s capital city. She also has a Diploma in Legal Practice from the Kampala Law Development Centre.

Her Master of Laws in trade and investment law was received at the University of Pretoria in South Africa. She also has a Masters in Public Administration from Harvard University in Cambridge, Massachusetts, USA. In June 2018, she received an honorary degree in law from Concordia University.

Clare Akamanzi was named CEO of NBA Africa on the 27th of December 2023. She will begin her tenure on the 23rd of January 2024.
