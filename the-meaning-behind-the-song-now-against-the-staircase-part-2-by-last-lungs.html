The Song’s Meaning

Last Lungs’ “Now Against The Staircase, Part 2” is a song that evokes deep emotions and touches the soul with its poignant lyrics and haunting melody. While the exact meaning may vary depending on the listener’s interpretation, the song appears to speak about a clandestine encounter or forbidden love. It paints vivid imagery of two individuals finding solace in each other’s arms, hidden away from the prying eyes of society.

In the opening lines, the lyrics describe the protagonists stepping out of the failing light, suggesting a departure from the ordinary world. They navigate a hallway with determination, shedding their inhibitions and clothing along the way. The imagery of torn-out paper lantern boats represents their vulnerability and fragility as they venture into unknown territory. The darkness they enter symbolizes secrecy and the allure of the forbidden.

The reference to a “balustrade of bones” suggests that they seek support and stability in a realm where their love may not be readily accepted. The floorboards moaning under the weight of their steps is a metaphor for the burden they carry as they navigate the complexities of their relationship. They are locked in a dance between desire and societal expectations.

The repetition of “Breath left in the turning night to ask our names” signifies the urgency and intensity of their passion. It is as if the night itself is whispering their names, urging them to acknowledge their desires. The line “Our skin is braille and what it says is nothing but a sigh” implies that their bodies communicate a language all their own, one that transcends verbal expression. Their connection is so profound that words are inadequate to capture its essence.

Personal Connection

As a music enthusiast, I have always believed that songs have the power to evoke emotions and transport us to different realms. “Now Against The Staircase, Part 2” by Last Lungs is no exception. Whenever I listen to this song, I am captivated by its somber beauty and the raw emotions it evokes.

The lyrics resonate with me personally as they remind me of a time when I found myself entangled in a prohibited love affair. The secrecy and intensity of the relationship created a sense of urgency and exhilaration, and the lyrics of this song perfectly capture those emotions. The imagery of shedding inhibitions and entering the darkness mirrors the experiences I had, where we had to conceal our love from the judgmental eyes of society.

Furthermore, the haunting melody and the band’s melancholic vocals add an extra layer of depth to the song’s overall atmosphere. It creates an ethereal and introspective ambiance that allows me to reflect on my own past and the emotions I experienced.

In conclusion, “Now Against The Staircase, Part 2” by Last Lungs is a song filled with symbolism and emotional depth. Its enigmatic lyrics paint a picture of forbidden love and encapsulate the intense emotions that accompany such relationships. Personally, this song holds a special place in my heart as it reflects a period of my life filled with passion, secrecy, and a sense of both longing and liberation.
