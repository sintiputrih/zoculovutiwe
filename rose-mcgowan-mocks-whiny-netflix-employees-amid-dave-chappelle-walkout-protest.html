Rose McGowan Mocks 'Whiny' Netflix Employees Amid Dave Chappelle Walkout Protest

Rose McGowan is not impressed by the Netflix employees staging a walkout.

On Wednesday (October 20), hundreds of Netflix employees, organized under a group called “Team Trans,” walked out of work in protest of the controversial Dave Chappelle special, calling for the streamer to remove it due to transphobic jokes.

The group also aimed for the walkout to “underscore the importance of responsible content offerings that prioritize the safety and dignity of all marginalized communities,” via CBS.

The 48-year-old actress spoke out on Twitter about the protest.

“1) Dear Whiny U.S. @Netflix Employees, I hear your feelings got hurt by Dave Chapelle & upset @JoeRogan didn’t get your fancy schools language memo. Piss off you brats. Enough of your fake activism & obnoxious self-importance,” she wrote.

“Do any of you twerps even know your paycheck comes from the deeply corrupt? Your bosses are DOJ/CIA stooges in on smearing [Julian] Assange in a fake documentary meant to misinform. You little Taylors risk NOTHING. It is Zero Hour. Get real or f–k off. Right now it’s about facts not your f–king feelings. ??” she continued.
