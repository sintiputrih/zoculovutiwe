In September Brielle told fans she’d undergone a double jaw surgery to correct her ‘TMJ and 9mm overjet overbite.’

As she recovered she updated fans on her progress and detailed the strain it put on her everyday life.

She noted she was optimistic she would be back to normal in time to enjoy Thanksgiving, and it appears she’s right on track.
