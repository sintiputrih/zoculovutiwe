Rich The Kid | Lifestyle & Vacation

Lifestyle

Multi-millionaire rapper Rich The Kid lives a luxurious lifestyle with a net worth of $10 million.

Even though he lives a luxurious lifestyle, flaunting jewelry and driving expensive vehicles, he has had several financial blunders over the years.

Blueprint Artist Management filed a lawsuit against the rapper in 2020, claiming that he owed them $3.5 million in fees.

According to another story, the rapper owed more than $300,000 in rent in Los Angeles, California.

He was also sued for owing jewels worth $230,000 to Peter Marco, a jeweler in Beverly Hills.

Talking about his personal life, Rich The Kid was formerly married to dancer Antonette Willis but ultimately decided not to stay married.

The couple had two children throughout their relationship.

Willis filed for divorce in March 2018 and requested sole physical custody while granting him visitation and joint legal custody.

This happened shortly after Willis claimed he cheated on her with Blac Chyna and India Love.

Later, Antonette alleged that Rich the Kid had abused her and often forced her to have abortions, claiming that calls to the police for domestic abuse at their home were routine.

A short while after the divorce was filed, the rapper started dating the model Tori Brixx.

However, it ended in 2021.

Furthermore, Rich The Kid was involved in a UTV accident in Calabasas, California, when he and his friend were riding around in a Polaris RZR 4-wheeler on December 29, 2018.

Moreover, he was robbed and fired at in West Hollywood, California, outside the Westlake Recording Studio in February 2019.

Rich The Kid | Charity

He once more partnered with Migos to release “Streets On Lock 4” in October 2015.
