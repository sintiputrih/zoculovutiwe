Emma Grede - Age, Family, Bio

Businesswoman and entrepreneur who is recognized for serving as the co-founder and CEO of Good American. She is also a founding partner of Skims, and a co-founder of Safely. She boasts more than 230,000 followers on her verified Instagram account.

Before Fame

She graduated from the London College of Fashion in 2001. 

Trivia

She worked as a producer at the fashion show and events company Inca Productions. She proposed an idea for a denim company to Kris Jenner. In 2021, she was a guest Shark on the series Shark Tank.

Family Life

She and her husband, Jens Grede, have four children. 

Associated With

Both she and Kim Kardashian are founding partners of Skims.
