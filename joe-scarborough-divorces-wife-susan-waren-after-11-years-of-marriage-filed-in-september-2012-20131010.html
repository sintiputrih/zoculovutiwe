Joe Scarborough Divorced Susan Waren After 11 Years, Filed in 2012

Looks like Joe Scarborough is a bachelor once again. After more than a decade of marriage, the Morning Joe host has quietly divorced second wife Susan Waren, TMZ reports.

In fact, the couple have been apart for quite some time. According to the site, the MSNBC star, 50, filed divorce documents in Connecticut in September 2012, just shy of the couple's 11th wedding anniversary in October. The split was finalized back in January.
