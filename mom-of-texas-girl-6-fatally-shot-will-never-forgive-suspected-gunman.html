Mom of Texas girl, 6, fatally shot will never forgive suspected gunman

The distraught mom of a 6-year-old girl shot dead when a fight broke out at a Texas car club meetup says she knows the murder suspect — and “will never forgive” him.

“She wasn’t doing anything wrong! She was enjoying herself,” she said.
