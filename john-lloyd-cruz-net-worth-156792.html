About

Alumnus of Batch 5 of of ABS-CBN's Star Magic. He has starred in the soap operas It Might Be You, Maging Sino Ka Man, and Imortal. In 2017, he began appearing as Mateo Rodriguez in La Luna Sangre.

Before Fame

Discovered at a mall by an ABS-CBN scout, he joined the show Star Magic, and together with Marc Solis and Baron Geisler - the Koolits - went to star in a number of films and TV shows.

Trivia

In addition to his acting role, he became a model and spokesperson for Nescafe, Libresse, and Smart Talk N Text.

Family Life

He was born to Luisito and Aida Espidol Cruz, and was named after St. John the Baptist. He has older siblings.

Associated With

He starred opposite Angel Locsin in the series Imortal, a dark fantasy romance on ABS-CBN.
