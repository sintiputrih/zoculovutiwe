Ryan paid me Errol Spence opines on Ryan Garcia honoring the bet on Rolly Romero vs Tank Davis

Errol Spence Jr. has just won a huge wad of cash from fellow boxer Ryan Garcia! Seems like Spence is undefeated both in and outside of the boxing ring! Ryan Garcia had to pay up a whopping $20,000 in cash to Spence Jr. after he lost a bet!

The bet in question was made ringside between Errol Spence and Ryan Garcia. Garcia was rooting for Rolando Romero during the clash with Gervonta Davis. Davis won the fight in the 6th round, handing out a loss to both his in-ring opponent Romero and Ryan Garcia via his pocket! A lot of bets are made and never paid! But it seems like Ryan Garcia is a man of his word! Errol Spence tweeted to let the whole world know that Ryan paid him his bet money!
