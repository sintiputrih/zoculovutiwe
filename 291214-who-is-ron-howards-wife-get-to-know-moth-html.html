Who Is Ron Howards Wife? Get To Know the Mother of His 4 Children

Ron Howard has marked his success as an actor and director in the entertainment industry. Behind him stands a great woman, his wife Cheryl, who is a star in her own right.

Ron Howard has cemented his name in Hollywood as a famous director with several accomplishments under his belt.

His most recent achievement was a National Geographic documentary, "We Feed People," that follows the remarkable journey of Spanish chef José Andrés towards becoming a humanitarian.

Ron Howard and Cheryl Howard during the 2018 Vanity Fair Oscar Party hosted by Radhika Jones at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on March 4, 2018 in Beverly Hills, California. | Source: Getty Images

The film streaming on Disney+ has a high chance of being nominated for an Emmy award in multiple categories. Should the production win, it will be among the many accolades the Oscar Award-winning director will receive.

Behind such a man stands a great woman, his wife, Cheryl Howard, who has been a source of constant support and happiness.

WHO IS CHERYL HOWARD?

Born on December 23, 1953, Cheryl was one of three children. She is also in the entertainment industry as an actress, appearing in the films "Night Shift," "Splash," "Willow," "Apollo 13," "A Beautiful Mind," "The Da Vinci Code," and "Angels & Demons."

Ron and Cheryl Howard at a press reception on September 4, 1975. | Source: Getty Images

She is also a talented author known for her books "Grandma, Tell Me a Story," "In the Face of Jinn," and "Heart & Desire." About writing, she said, "I didn't start writing until the age of 22. English was never my strong point, but I do dearly love a good yarn."

She married her high school sweetheart, Ron, in 1975 and has since maintained their incredible love for each other. With over 40 years of marriage, many have asked the couple their secret to a long-lasting union. Ron said:

"There's no tactic other than communication is really important. You have to learn to communicate and have difficult conversations in constructive ways."

Ron Howard and Cheryl Howard during the 2018 Vanity Fair Oscar Party hosted by Radhika Jones at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on March 04, 2018 in Beverly Hills, California. | Source: Getty Images

Ron has gushed about his wife several times, giving her credit for keeping things together during difficult times. Although their relationship didn't go without problems, the couple always knew their love was stronger than the hurdles. In a 2013 interview, he said:

"I felt really lucky when we met. It's crazy. We were teenagers. It shouldn't have worked. We got married young [and] that shouldn't have worked either, and yet it really and truly has."

HOW MANY CHILDREN DOES RON HOWARD HAVE?

The couple shares four children, Bryce, Paige, Jocelyn, and Reed. Bryce has followed in her parents' footsteps as an actress, known for her appearance in the "Twilight" saga.

Paige Howard and Ron Howard during the 2019 Vanity Fair Oscar Party hosted by Radhika Jones at Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts on February 24, 2019 in Beverly Hills, California. | Source: Getty Images

Meanwhile, her twin sisters, Paige and Jocely, have pursued different paths from each other. While Paige also became an actress, Jocelyn chose a career away from the spotlight. Although, she did appear in one "Arrested Development" episode.

Their youngest brother, Reed, has chosen an athletic career. He is a professional golf player and a YouTuber, sharing content about his sport. His official YouTube channel is Reed Howard Golf.

Although Ron and Cheryl's children pursued different paths in life, they remain close to each other, as evident through Instagram posts.
