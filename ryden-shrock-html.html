Ryden Shrock - Age, Family, Bio

Best recognized as the son of YouTube skateboarding and comedy sensation Andy Schrock. He is a frequent star on his father's self-titled channel and a member of the web group TurboToyTime. The web group has featured him unboxing and reviewing toys. 

Before Fame

On January 28, 2012, he made his YouTube debut in his father's video "Today, My Son Was Born."

Trivia

He is the star of one of his father's most popular YouTube videos "How To Play With Your Toddler 101: PlayGround!" which earned more than 37 million views in just over a year. He was also featured in a July 2017 video that showed him doing a "CAKE-ATHLON" obstacle course with his dad. 

Family Life

His parents celebrated their three year wedding anniversary in July of 2016.

Associated With

In 2014, he met skateboarding legend Tony Hawk in the YouTube video "2 Year Old Skater Meets Tony Hawk!"
