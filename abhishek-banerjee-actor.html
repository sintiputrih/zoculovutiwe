Wiki/Biography

Abhishek Banerjee was born on Tuesday, 15 November 1988 (age 31 years; as in 2019), in New Delhi. His zodiac sign is Scorpio. He did his schooling from KV Andrews Ganj in New Delhi. He attended Kirori Mal College (KMC), Delhi to complete his graduation. [1]

Career

He started his career as a theatre actor. He joined a theatre group in Delhi. He got his first break as an actor in the Bollywood film, ‘Rang De Basanti’ (2006); as one of the students auditioning for a documentary role.

Abhishek Banerjee in Rang De Basanti

In 2016, he got immense popularity with The Screen Patti’s YouTube video, ‘Daaru Pe Charcha- Vijay Maal Legaya’ along with Jitendra Kumar aka Jeetu Bhaiya.

He has worked as an actor and casting director in various Bollywood films, like ‘No One Killed Jessica’ (2011), ‘The Dirty Picture’ (2011), ‘Do Lafzon Ki Kahani’ (2016), ‘Secret Superstar’ (2017), and ‘Kalank’ (2019). He is well-appreciated for his acting skills in many popular Hindi web-series, like ‘TVF Pitchers’ (2015), ‘Mirzapur’ (2018), ‘Typewriter’ (2019), ‘Mirzapur Season 2’ (2020), and ‘Paatal Lok’ (2020).
