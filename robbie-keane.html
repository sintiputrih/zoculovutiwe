Synopsis

A proven striker, Robbie Keane has scored more than 300 goals in his professional career. He is a professional footballer from Ireland. He last played for LA Galaxy. He played as a forward. He served as the captain of the Republic of Ireland national team from March 2006 to August 2016. Currently, he retired from the International format of the football.
