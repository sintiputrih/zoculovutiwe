About

Known for his role as Roman Godfrey in the Netflix original horror series Hemlock Grove, this Swedish-born screen actor also played the title role in the 2010 comedy movie Simple Simon. His other screen credits include The Crown Jewels (Swedish title: Kronjuvelerna) and a 2012 English film version of Anna Karenina. He was cast as Pennywise in the 2017 remake of the horror flick It.
