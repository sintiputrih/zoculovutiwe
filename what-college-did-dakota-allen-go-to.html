What college did Dakota Allen go to?

Dakota Allen is a talented football player who gained recognition through his participation in the popular Netflix series "Last Chance U." Known for his exceptional skills on the field, Dakota Allen's journey from high school to college has been an inspiring one. In this article, we will explore the college Dakota Allen attended and delve into his experiences as a college athlete.

Dakota Allen's Background

Before we dive into his college journey, let's take a quick look at Dakota Allen's background. Born on November 6, 1995, in Humble, Texas, Allen developed a passion for football at a young age. He attended Summer Creek High School, where he played as a linebacker and showcased his exceptional talent on the field.

The Journey to College

After graduating from high school, Dakota Allen faced an unexpected setback. In 2015, during his freshman year, Allen was dismissed from his college football team, Texas Tech University, due to his involvement in a burglary incident. This incident could have derailed his football career, but Allen was determined to make a comeback.

East Mississippi Community College

In his quest to get back on track, Dakota Allen made a pivotal decision to enroll at East Mississippi Community College (EMCC) in Scooba, Mississippi. EMCC is renowned for its successful football program and has gained widespread recognition through the Netflix docuseries "Last Chance U."

"Last Chance U"

The Netflix series "Last Chance U" follows the football team at EMCC, showcasing the lives and struggles of talented athletes seeking a second chance to prove themselves. Dakota Allen became a central figure in the show during its second season in 2016. The series highlighted his journey of redemption and showcased his exceptional football skills.

EMCC Football Program

EMCC's football program has a history of producing talented players who go on to succeed at the collegiate and professional levels. Under the guidance of head coach Buddy Stephens, the Lions have won multiple national championships. Dakota Allen's decision to join EMCC allowed him to be part of this renowned program and gain exposure to college recruiters.

Redemption and Success

During his time at EMCC, Dakota Allen not only excelled on the field but also demonstrated personal growth and maturity. He embraced the opportunity for redemption, working diligently to overcome his past mistakes. Allen's dedication and talent caught the attention of college recruiters, paving the way for his return to a Division I football program.

Texas Tech University

After a successful season at EMCC, Dakota Allen received offers from several Division I universities. In 2017, he made the decision to return to Texas Tech University, the same institution he was dismissed from just a few years earlier. This decision marked a significant milestone in his journey of redemption.

College Football Career

Dakota Allen's return to Texas Tech University was marked by outstanding performances on the football field. As a linebacker, he became a crucial component of the Red Raiders' defense. Allen's exceptional tackling technique, football IQ, and leadership skills helped him become one of the standout players on the team.

NFL Draft and Professional Career

Following his successful college career, Dakota Allen declared for the NFL Draft in 2019. He was selected by the Los Angeles Rams in the seventh round, becoming the 251st overall pick. Allen's journey from being dismissed from Texas Tech to getting drafted in the NFL served as a testament to his resilience, determination, and growth as a person and athlete.

Dakota Allen's college journey is a testament to the power of second chances and personal growth. After being dismissed from Texas Tech University, he enrolled at East Mississippi Community College, where he showcased his skills and earned a second chance at a Division I program.
