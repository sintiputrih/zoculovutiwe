VIDEO Holly Madison pregnant with 2nd child: watch her sonogram!

2½-year-old Rainbow Aurora Rotella is going to be a big sister! Rainbow’s mom Holly Madison has confirmed that she is currently three months pregnant with her and husband Pasquale Rotella’s second child.

“I’m so excited,” the former Girls Next Door star tells People. “This time I know what to expect a little bit more. [That] makes it even more enjoyable!”

Sophomore pop-to-be Pasquale took to Instagram and shared a video of Holly’s sonogram in which you can clearly hear the baby’s “strong” heartbeat:

As far as the sex of the new baby, Holly says they don’t know yet. “I’m kind of feeling the boy vibe,” she says. “We’ll see how psychic I am!”

One person who is hoping Holly’s psychic game is not on fleek is Rainbow. “She’s rooting for a girl,” Holly reveals. “I think she’s going to be a good big sister. She’s very caring and she loves playing with her baby dolls and taking care of the pets.”

Holly and Pasquale caused quite the stir with their daughter’s now-famous name (find out the reason why they named her Rainbow Aurora here!), so are they planning on a more conventional moniker this time around? Not likely. “I don’t really like normal names,” Holly says, adding that she and Pasquale already “have a few finalists” picked out.
