Dana York Bio, Age, Wiki, Everything About Tom Pettys Ex-Wife

Dana was a very supportive wife who stood by the side of her husband throughout the time, he was struggling with drug addiction and she finally helped him to overcome it. Dana York eventually quit her job after her marriage to Petty in order to focus on her family life.

During that time, she became her husband’s musical band scheduling manager and was in charge of the scheduling of the band’s musical tours and concerts.

Dana York’s Husband

York’s husband was born as Thomas Earl Petty in October 1950. He was an American musician, songwriter, record producer, and an actor as well.
