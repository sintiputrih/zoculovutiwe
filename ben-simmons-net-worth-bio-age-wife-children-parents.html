Ben Simmons, also known as Benjamin David Simmons, is a professional basketball player from Australia who plays for the Philadelphia 76ers of the National Basketball Association (NBA).

A first-team All-American and the USBWA National Freshman of the Year, he played collegiate basketball for the Louisiana State University (LSU) Tigers for one season.

The 76ers selected Simmons with the first overall choice in the 2016 NBA Draft.

He was voted the NBA Rookie of the Year in 2018 after missing a year due to a damaged right foot. 2019–2021 saw Simmons selected as an NBA All-Star.
