Amazons 8 Coziest New Arrivals Include Lounge Sets and Sweater Dresses From $15

October is here which means I’m wholeheartedly embracing all things fall — and my style choices are no exception. Luckily, Amazon’s major autumn savings event is right around the corner, and you don’t even have to wait until then to snag epic markdowns on pieces you’ll wear all season; the retailer already released thousands of new fashion finds with prices as low as just $15. 

To make your fall wardrobe refresh a breeze, I sifted through the impressive selection of styles and narrowed down the eight best finds. Below, shop chunky cardigans, two-piece lounge sets, chic sweater dresses, and stylish outerwear, all for less than $40. And, be sure to grab your favorite fall-perfect pieces while they’re still available ahead of Amazon’s Prime Big Deal Days next week.  

Best New Amazon Fashion Arrivals:

Lillusory Collared Cardigan

What’s fall without cozy knitwear? Nothing compares to snuggling up in a sweater on a brisk autumn day, so I’m snagging this Lillusory cardigan while it’s still marked down to $24 with its on-site coupon. The new arrival has a relaxed fit for an extra comfortable feel, yet it can easily be dressed up thanks to its stylish detailing. It’s made with a structured collar, subtly flared sleeves, and a chic, ribbed texture. You can snag the sweater in five versatile neutral tones, and it’s available in sizes S through XL. I plan to wear mine solo with jeans and boots, and layered open over dresses and blouses all season.  

Ekouaer Two-Piece Lounge Set

Trust me, if I could live in my pajamas, I would. So, for something a bit more elevated, I opt for the next best thing: lounge sets. The comfy outfit formula practically becomes my daily uniform once fall rolls around, and this year I can’t help but add this Ekouaer find to my cart. The coordinating set is complete with drawstring, wide-leg pants and an oversized, V-neck sweater; and both pieces come out to $34 — aka just $17 each. It’s sold in a range of colorways including deep autumnal tones and bright, Barbie pink. What really sold me on the set is the number of ways it can be styled; wear the pieces individually, pair them with slippers around the house, or upgrade the entire look with accessories and outerwear.   

Merokeety Long Sweater Dress

No autumn wardrobe is complete without the perfect sweater dress, and this Merokeety find is just that. It’s made of a stretchy and soft blend of rayon and polyester for a cozy yet breathable feel. Plus, it has a flattering, snug silhouette and a dramatic, maxi-length hemline. My personal favorite element of the piece is its collared, button-down design which adds an extra layer of sophistication and style. The long-sleeve, ribbed dress is available in nine colorways, and I can’t resist buying this chic, khaki option while it’s just $29.
