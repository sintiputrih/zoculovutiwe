Career

Ciara is a Grammy Award-winning singer, record producer, songwriter, and actress from the United States. She began to dominate the spotlight following the release of her debut single and album, both titled Goodies. During her adolescence, she was a member of the girl group Hearsay, and after the group disbanded, she pursued a solo music career. Her debut single, Goodies, was a commercial and critical success, topping the US Billboard Hot 100 chart for seven weeks and reaching the top of the UK Singles Chart. She then followed up with her debut album of the same name. The album was a huge success, earning her two Grammy nominations.
