Chrissy Teigens Daughter, Luna, Wins Halloween: All Her Costumes

Chrissy Teigen and John Legend‘s eldest daughter, Luna, always has the cutest Halloween costumes, from a hot dog to a hula dancer.

Luna, who was born in April 2016, is no stranger to extravagant outfits and has been known to casually wear princess dresses around the house. Teigen exclusively told Us Weekly in March 2019 that her daughter was “so into princesses … and Disney movies,” which inspired their plans for her birthday that year.

“I think we’re just gonna keep it small, keep it family, and make her Disney dreams come true,” she said at the time. “I think we’ve been to enough kids’ birthday parties here in L.A. to know that, ‘OK, we can’t do this.’”

After Luna’s arrival, Teigen and Legend welcomed son Miles in 2018. Two years later, Teigen announced in September 2020 that she suffered a pregnancy loss with son Jack due to partial placenta abruption. The couple have since welcomed daughter Esti in January 2023 and son Wren via surrogate in June 2023.
