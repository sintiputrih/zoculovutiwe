David Zuckerman Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

David J. Zuckerman (born August 28, 1962) is an American writer and producer who is best known as the creator of the animated comedy series Family Guy and as the showrunner and executive producer of the American adaptation of the Australian television series of the same name, Wilfred. Zuckerman was born in New York City and raised in Los Angeles. Zuckerman is a native of Danville, California, and a graduate of the University of California, Los Angeles. He is married with two children. At first, Zuckerman worked as a writer for the comedy The Fresh Prince of Bel-Air, and later as a producer for the Fox animated series King of the Hill, which he created and directed. His collaboration with writer and producer Seth MacFarlane resulted in the creation of a second animated series for Fox in 1999 named Family Guy. Following that, he worked on MacFarlane’s second series, American Dad!, before adapting the Australian live-action series Wilfred and producing it under the same name for the FX television network.
