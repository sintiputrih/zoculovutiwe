Priyanka Chopra Announces Engagement to Nick Jonas with Instagram Photo of Engagement Ring

Priyanka Chopra and Nick Jonas are totally in love, and now there's an Instagram to prove it. Amid reports that Jonas is in India with his parents right now to meet Chopra's family, the Quantico star has declared her love for the singer on social media.

Related Story

In the adorable post, Chopra and Jonas gaze adoringly into one another's eyes, and that all-important engagement ring is crystal clear in the photo. With the location showing as Mumbai, Chopra writes, "Taken.. With all my heart and soul.." meaning that the wedding is most definitely on, and the actress is completely smitten.

Plus, if the rumors are to be believed, the pair could be getting married very soon.

As if Chopra's post wasn't perfect enough, Jonas commented on the photo with his own cheeky remark writing, "Wow congrats. He's the luckiest guy in the world." Basically, the singer wants the world to know that he's serious about his new fiancée.

Instagram

And to make your heart totally melt, Jonas posted the same photo on Instagram, along with the caption, "Future Mrs. Jonas. My heart. My love." I'm officially dead:

Earlier this week, a source told E! that "All of the plans are moving quickly and Priyanka and Nick do not want to have a long engagement."

Chopra's gorgeous engagement ring reportedly cost $200,000, and it was reported that Jonas even closed down a Tiffany & Co. store to pick out the perfect diamond.

An insider close to the couple reportedly told E! News, "Everyone is happy. They are definitely a great match." And judging by the fast pace with which their relationship is moving, Chopra and Jonas could be heading down the aisle very soon.
