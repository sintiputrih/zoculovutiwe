What Happened To Owen Wilson's Nose?

When people look back on Owen Wilson's career decades from now, they will likely be very impressed with everything he has accomplished.

An extremely popular comedic actor, Wilson has starred in several movies that millions of people love and have watched multiple times. On top of that, cinephiles will likely remember that Wilson co-wrote some of the movies that made him a star.

Related: Owen Wilson Makes A Surprising Amount Of Money For This Catchphrase

Despite all of those accomplishments and the fact that Wilson is now part of the MCU, there is no denying that two silly things are a big part of his legacy.

The first silly but admittedly very entertaining thing about Wilson that will likely be remembered is his popular catchphrase, "wow". After all, Wilson says "wow" in such a uniquely musical way that fans simply can't get enough of him saying that word.

The other thing that a lot of people will remember about Wilson is his distinctive nose. However, even though Wilson's nose has been a topic of discussion for many years, the fact is that many of his fans have no idea why he looks like that.

Since Wilson has been a movie star for many years, he has taken part in hundreds, if not thousands of interviews during his life. Unsurprisingly, the topic of Wilson's nose has come up in some of those interviews.

Most notably, Wilson explained how he got his distinctive nose during a 2001 Los Angeles Times interview.

As Wilson revealed during that interview, when he was a teen attending St. Mark's High School in Dallas, Texas, he was in a fight when he suffered an injury to his nose. However, since then, Wilson has described the fight as more akin to roughhousing with a friend.

As Wilson also revealed during the aforementioned 2001 interview, his nose got struck again when he was playing football in his youth. Those two injuries combined caused his nose to look the way it does now.

Many years later in 2022, Wilson took part in a Wired Autocomplete Interview in which he yet again explained what happened to his nose. At that time, he added a fascinating element to the story, the aforementioned game where he reinjured his nose was a flag football game.

Once you know the story of what happened to Wilson's nose, it is amazing that he suffered two serious injuries during incidents that sound relatively innocent.

What Happened To Jennifer Grey's Nose?

Years before people started talking about Owen Wilson's nose, there was another movie star who was discussed because of that part of their body.

In 1996 and 1997, Jennifer Grey starred in Ferris Bueller's Day Off and Dirty Dancing respectively. After both of those movies became hits that have gone down in history, many people thought Grey's career had a lot of potential.

After Grey rose to fame, she decided to get a pair of rhinoplasty surgeries. If Grey got those nose jobs because she wanted to feel more self-confident, then that would have been great.

Sadly, Grey has revealed that she changed her nose after her mom advised her that doing so may make it easier for her to land roles. That said, it is important to note that Grey described her mom as loving and supportive in her memoir. Grey also made it clear that her mom always loved the way Jennifer looked, she was just trying to be pragmatic.

After Grey got her nose surgeries, many fans found her unrecognizable. In fact, even some of Grey's fans didn't recognize Jennifer when she appeared in the popular sitcom Friends.

Years later when Grey released her memoir, she wrote about regretting her rhinoplasty surgeries. “Overnight, I lose my identity and my face,”

Grey also described the time an airport worker who had Jennifer's paperwork reacted to her name. “‘Oh, Jennifer Grey, like the actress.’” According to Grey, when she told the airport employee that she was the Dirty Dancing star, they refused to believe her. “‘I’ve seen ‘Dirty Dancing a dozen times. I know Jennifer Grey. And you are not her.’”

Based on what happened to Grey, it seems very clear that Owen Wilson has been wise to embrace his nose.
