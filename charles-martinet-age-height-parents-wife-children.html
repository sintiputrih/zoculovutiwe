Charles Martinet Age, Height, Parents, Wife, Children

Charles Martinet is an American actor and voice actor best known for his portrayal of both Mario and Luigi in the Super Mario video game series since 1992.

He is also the voice of other characters in the series such as Wario and Waluigi.

Martinet’s official debut as Mario was in the 1992 Super Mario Bros. pinball machine, despite being uncredited for the role.

However, most were first exposed to Mario’s voice in the landmark 1996 game Super Mario 64.
