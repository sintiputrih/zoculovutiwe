Brad Maddox -- My Post-WWE Name Is ...

Originally published on ProWrestlingSheet.

Former RAW General Manager Brad Maddox has a new name lined up for his post-WWE career -- and honestly, it's even better than the one he had before.

Sources close to Maddox tell us the wrestler will now be using the name Mad Braddox. In conjunction with the name change, we're told he'll also be launching a ProWrestlingTees.com store tomorrow afternoon featuring 3 shirt designs.

Still no word on where or when he plans to wrestle following his release from WWE last week ... but with a new name and store, it looks like we haven't seen the last of Brad Maddox.
