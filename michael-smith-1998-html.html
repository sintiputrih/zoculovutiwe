Michael Smith (Instagram Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram star and YouTube influencer who is best recognized for his Natural Physique and personal training content. He shares videos of his fitness routines and workout exercises through Instagram for his over 360,000 followers. 

Before Fame

He first started gaining attention when he posted fitness photos through Instagram in March of 2017. He joined YouTube that same time and by 2019 had earned over 43 million views. 

Trivia

He is an ambassador for both Gym Shark and EHP Labs. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in Akron, Ohio. 

Associated With

He shared a photo with fellow fitness influencer Zac Perna through his Instagram in November of 2019.
