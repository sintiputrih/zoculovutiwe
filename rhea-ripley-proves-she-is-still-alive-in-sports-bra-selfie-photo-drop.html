Rhea Ripley Proves She Is Still Alive In Sports Bra Selfie Photo Drop

Rhea Ripley has been one of the mainstays of WWE’s women’s division for quite some time. Ripley made an impact on NXT UK, collected the top title in NXT, and became a top star on the main roster.

Ripley took to Twitter on Wednesday and posted a couple of mirror selfies. The former Raw Women’s Champion showed off her new haircut and also posted an update about her health.

I’m Alive

Ripley was originally set to face Bianca Belair for the Raw Women’s Championship at Money in the Bank this Saturday, but had to be pulled out of the match due to brain injury.

WWE knew they needed to pull Ripley from the match weeks ago. Therefore, the decision to pull Ripley from the match was one they knew was coming.
