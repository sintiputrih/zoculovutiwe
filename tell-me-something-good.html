Overall Meaning

The song "Tell Me Something Good" by Rufus is a passionate plea for genuine emotion and connection over empty words and meaningless actions. The lyrics tell the story of a person who is tired of being with someone who is emotionally closed off and puts their pride before everything else. The singer assures this person that they have something within themselves that can set them on fire, that can make them feel something real. The singer is trying to get this person to open up, to tell them something good, something that will bring them closer together.


The repetition of the phrase "Tell me something good" throughout the song emphasizes the desperation of the singer. They want nothing more than to connect with this person on a deeper level and feel something genuine. The line "Your problem is you ain't been loved like you should" reflects the singer's understanding of the root of their partner's emotional distance. The song is ultimately about the healing power of love and the importance of connecting on a real level.


Overall, "Tell Me Something Good" is a powerful and passionate plea for genuine connection over empty words and meaningless actions.
