Summer Walker tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

R&B artist, Summer Walker, is going on a well-anticipated 2022 tour.

Fans can buy tickets now to see the Girls Need Love singer.

How can I buy tickets for the Summer Walker tour?

Summer Walker tour tickets are currently on sale by ticket vendors including; StubHub, SeatGeek and Ticketmaster.

She will be visiting cities like Houston and Dallas, Texas, Chicago, Illinois, Sacramento, California, Washington D.C., and Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.

The tour will also include two nights in the UK - London and Birmingham.

Who is Summer Walker?

Summer Marjani Walker, 25, was born on April 11, 1996, in Atlanta, Georgia. 

She had to work as a cleaner and stripper in her home city for nearly two years before reaching success.

Before her time at the top, she taught herself how to play guitar by watching tutorials on YouTube.  

Talent and luck combined when a woman who works as the studio manager of a label LVRN discovered her.

Summer is now signed to Love Renaissance and Interscope Records.

She and rapper, London on da Track, 30, welcomed their daughter in March 2021.

The pair are no longer together.

Near the mid-2021, the singer revealed she was dating rapper, LVRD Pharaoh Larry.

In November that same year, the couple debuted face tattoos with each others names.

What is Summer Walker's net worth?

Walker's net worth is estimated to be around $4million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Walker is best known for her commercial mixtape Last Day Of Summer, which was released in October 2018.

Her single, Girls Need Love, became her first top 40 entry on the Billboard Hot 100 chart following a remix featuring Drake.
