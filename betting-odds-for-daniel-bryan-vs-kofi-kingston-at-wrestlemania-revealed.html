Betting Odds For Daniel Bryan vs Kofi Kingston At WrestleMania Revealed

As seen on Sky Bet, the betting odds for Daniel Bryan vs Kofi Kingston at WrestleMania 35 have been released.

The rise of KofiMania over the course of the last few months has been nothing short of phenomenal, with fans getting behind Kingston like they never have before. He’s had a great run in WWE up to this point, but now, he’s finally getting the chance to show the world what he can do on the biggest stage of them all.

On the opposite end of the spectrum you’ve got Daniel Bryan, who is putting in some of the best work of his career as the pompous heel in this feud. When you combine those two elements, it’s impossible not to imagine this being a barnburner of a match this Sunday.

Kofi fans will be happy to learn that the man himself is indeed the favourite to become a World Champion for the first time in his career, with odds of 1/3. Bryan is the 9/4 underdog to retain his belt against the New Day member.

Both men have big match experience, but in a way, nothing they’ve done in the past can really compare to this. Bryan had his big moment at WrestleMania 30 when he won the WWE World Heavyweight Championship, but this storyline has really helped him to prove what he can do outside the constraints of a babyface role.

Things are probably going to keep developing into the next few pay per views after WrestleMania between these two men, and if that’s the case, we couldn’t be happier.
