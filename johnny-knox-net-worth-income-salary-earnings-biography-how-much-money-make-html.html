Known professionally as Johnny Otis Knox II (born November 3, 1986), he was a former American football wide receiver who played three seasons in the National Football League (NFL). He was a member of the Abilene Christian University football team throughout his undergraduate years. In the 2009 NFL Draft, he was chosen by the Chicago Bears with the 140th overall selection in the first round. After suffering a severe spinal injury during a regular season game against the Seattle Seahawks in 2011, he made the decision to retire from the National Football League (NFL) on February 13, 2013.
