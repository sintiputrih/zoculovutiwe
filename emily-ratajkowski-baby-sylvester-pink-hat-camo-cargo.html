Emily Ratajkowski Braces the Snow in Pink Fuzzy Hat &amp; Camo Cargo

Snow days in New York City are an opportune time to show off your style. Case in point: Emily Ratajkowski (plus her adorable 10-month-old baby boy, Sylvester Apollo Bear) just wore a bold blizzard outfit.

Over the weekend, the My Body author shared new photos of her and Sylvester walking through the city's snow-covered sidewalks. EmRata appeared in a fuzzy pink bucket hat, an unlikely accessory to complement her baggy camouflage cargo pants. She also wore a pair of tan snow boots and a red hooded bomber jacket from Moose Knuckles.

Baby Sylvester wore an outfit with red highlights of his own, repping a bright-red beanie with a brown bear decal on it and a pair of Elmo shoes. EmRata also dressed him in a gray zip-up onesie featuring a pattern of yellow alien spaceships and red stars.

The model seems to have an affinity for the fuzzy hat trend during the cold-weather season. While promoting her memoir in London, she was spotted on two separate occasions wearing the fluffy toppers.

In one instance, Ratajkowski headed to London's Battersea Arts Centre wearing a matching fluffy coat and miniskirt in a zebra print, courtesy of the Michael Kors Collection, accessorizing with a black hat and red Jimmy Choo knee-high boots. Later, she was spotted after a book signing for My Body wearing a bold print with a fluffy cheetah-print hat. She tied the rest of the monochrome look together with a long black coat, black trousers, and black knee-high boots.
