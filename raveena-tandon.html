Wiki/Biography

Raveena Tandon Thadani was born on 26 October 1974 (age 47 years; as of 2021) in Mumbai. Her zodiac sign is Scorpio.

Raveena Tandon As A Child

She did her schooling at Mumbai’s Jamnabai Narsee School and went to Mithibai College to pursue her Bachelors in Arts. She had to drop out her college after 2 years due to her increasing popularity as a Bollywood actress. As a child, she wanted to be an IPS officer or a Pilot. Tandon never thought of becoming an actress. However, when she was in the middle of her internship at Genesis PR, she received a call from Shantanu Sheorey’s office saying that they wanted to shoot her for an advertisement. At the age of 15, she was offered an advertisement for Sunsilk which was her first project.

Career

Raveena made her acting debut in 1991 with the Bollywood film “Patthar Ke Phool.”

Raveena Tandon in Patthar Ke Phool

Her performance in the film was appraised by the audience and won her a Filmfare Award for Lux New Face of the Year. Subsequently, she featured in many hit films including Laadla (1994), Dilwale (1994), Mohra (1994), Khiladiyon Ka Khiladi (1996), Ziddi (1997), Bade Miyan Chote Miyan (1998) and Dulhe Raja (1998).

In 1999, she made her Kannada film debut with the movie “Upendra.”

In the 2000s, Raveena went to work in art films and realistic cinema which won her huge acclaims. In 2001, her performance in the film Daman: A Victim of Marital Violence bagged her a National Film Award for Best Actress. Her last appearance before she took a long break from acting was in the movie “Sandwich” in 2006. She made her Bollywood comeback with the film “Bombay Velvet” in 2015.

In addition to acting, Tandon has produced two films. She has hosted the TV shows “Isi Ka Naam Zindagi” and “Simply Baatien With Raveena.” She has also appeared as a judge on TV shows like “Sabse Bada Kalakar,” “The Drama Company,” and Nach Baliye 9.”

Controversies

  • During the ongoing #MeToo movement in India, Raveena Tandon tweeted in support of the movement after which a lot of Twitterati’s linked her tweet to her past relationship with Akshay Kumar. However, later, she slammed the Twitterati’s by saying that she did not tweet just because of her past life but because of what she had seen happening to the women in the industry. 
  • In 2018, When there were several strikes by the farmers going on in various states, Raveena tweeted that anyone who was harming public properties and commodities should be instantly arrested. After her tweets, she was trolled for her insensitivity and political leanings. She immediately deleted her post after that and tweeted that she never meant farmers and she was talking about antisocials. She also blamed media for distorting her comments.
  • In 2018, Raveena courted controversy after shooting for a commercial in ‘no camera zone’ at Lingaraj Temple in Odisha’s capital city of Bhubaneswar. The incident came into highlight after a clip featuring the Raveena giving beauty tips at Lingaraj Temple in Odisha’s capital city of Bhubaneswar was uploaded on the social media. An FIR was filed by the authorities of the temple against her.
  • In 2015, she sued matrimonial sites such as Shaadi.com and Shaaditimes.com for using her images for promotional causes without her permission.
