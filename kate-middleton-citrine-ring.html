Kate Middleton Citrine Ring - Kate Middleton Sported a Push Present from Prince William at Wimbledon

The Duchess of Cambridge was out and about this past week–between the christening of her youngest son, Prince Louis, last Monday and the various Wimbledon matches she attended this past weekend. Catherine was spotted in the royal box with Meghan Markle, the Duchess of Sussex, on Saturday morning for the Women's Championship match, where Serena Williams was defeated by Angelique Kerber, and joined her husband, Prince William on Sunday afternoon for the Men's final between Novak Djokovic and Kevin Anderson.

While the match was riveting, most were focused on the Duchess, who sported a bold and bright yellow dress by Dolce & Gabbana, and the most stunning accessory to match–a citrine ring gifted to her by Prince William after the birth of Prince Louis this past April.

Getty Images

Kate sported the ring on her right hand, with her wedding band and engagement ring–the iconic heirloom sapphire with a diamond halo that once belonged to Princess Diana–on her left. Citrine is said to be connected to warmth, comfort and a healing energy, as it reflects the power of the sunlight, making it an even more appropriate gift for a new mother. According to Hello!, Catherine added earrings and a necklace from Asprey to complete the look.

Catherine's various moments of concentration during the match gave onlookers the best shots of the newest addition to her accessory wardrobe. She was first spotted sporting her new ring at Prince Harry's wedding to Meghan Markle, which was the Duchess's first public appearance since the birth of Prince Louis only three weeks earlier. She wore the ring on her right hand for the Duke and Duchess of Sussex's ceremony, and paired it with an ivory Alexander McQueen coat and dress.

Getty Images

As Catherine has in the past, it is traditional for royals to receive push presents from their spouses. William gifted Catherine with a piece of jewelry to commemorate the birth of both Prince George and Princess Charlotte. In 2015, she received a pair of custom Kiki McDonough green tourmaline, green amethyst and diamond earrings after Charlotte's birth, as pictured on the designer's Instagram.

After Prince George's birth, the Duchess reportedly received an eternity band, which she wears alongside her engagement ring. It appears that in addition to his admirable charity work and being a fantastic father, the Duke of Cambridge also has another impressive talent: Prince William knows how to pick out a stellar piece of jewelry.
