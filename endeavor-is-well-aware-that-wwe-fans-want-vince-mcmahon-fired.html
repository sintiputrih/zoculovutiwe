Endeavor Is Well Aware That WWE Fans Want Vince McMahon Fired

Vince McMahon made his return to WWE, and it didn’t take long for him to regain his seat as Chairman of the company’s Board of Directors. Mr. McMahon ushered in a deal to sell his company, which resulted in a merger between WWE and the UFC. That being said, Endeavor is well aware how unhappy WWE fans are right now.

#FireVince trended in big way after Vince McMahon’s job with WWE RAW this week. There were also chants during NXT calling for the Chairman’s release.

Ringside News was told by a tenured source within WWE that the company’s new owners “are aware of every criticism against Vince McMahon.” This is pretty hard to ignore given the outcry from fans.

WWE fans had a lot to complain about when Vince McMahon made his big return to the company. That being said, the powers that be in this deal are well aware what the popular opinion is.

They’re aware of the scandal. They’re aware of all the criticism of Vince’s rewritten scripts and anything else you could think of.

Vince McMahon’s return to WWE was followed by a massive backlash from fans. He is not calling the shots in creative once again, and Endeavor is well aware that this is not the desired outcome for a lot of fans.

While Vince McMahon ran WWE RAW this week, slashing the script up as they went along during the live show, Triple H was given “busy work.” This change back to the old guard is not welcomed by fans, but it seems that Endeavor doesn’t care.
