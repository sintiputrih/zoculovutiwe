Obituary of Golf Enthusiast Mr. Roger Lundy

With Heavy Hearts, We Announce the Sudden Passing of Mr. Roger Lundy”

It is with profound sadness that we announce the sudden passing of Mr. Roger Lundy on the evening of October 30, 2023, in Greenwood, Indiana, after a brief illness.

Born and raised in Greenwood, Mr. Lundy dedicated over 20 years of his life to the sport of golf. With a deep love and passion for golf, he mentored, guided, and inspired countless generations of aspiring athletes to pursue their dreams.

Up until his final moments, he remained devoted to his coaching duties, diligently preparing the women’s golf team of Franklin University for a major tournament in Puerto Rico. His unwavering dedication, professionalism, and love for golf will forever serve as a shining example to his students and colleagues.

To his family, Mr. Lundy was a loving husband and an exceptional father, always caring and sharing. He leaves behind beautiful memories of family bonds and a sporting legacy for his wife and children. Though he has departed, his legacy of passion for golf and selfless dedication will live on in the hearts of all.

Details regarding the funeral arrangements will be announced later.

Roger Lundy Memorial Tribute

On October 30th, our golf community in Indiana and the town of Greenwood lost a revered member – Mr. Roger Lundy.

For over 40 years, in his relentless pursuit of a golf career, Mr. Lundy was an exemplary figure of dedication, professionalism, and boundless passion for the sport. As the President of the Indiana Golf Association and a coach at Franklin University, he mentored and inspired countless young athletes.

Until his final moments, Mr. Lundy remained unwavering in his commitment and responsibilities. His sacrificial spirit and unwavering dedication will continue to serve as a guiding light for generations to come. Though he has passed on, the flame of passion for golf that he ignited will continue to spread.

Our Indiana golf community will forever remember Mr. Roger Lundy – a legend, a devoted mentor, and a shining example of sporting passion. May he rest in peace on the peaceful greens of the golf course.
