Marvels Spider-Man 2: How to unlock the Black Panther (Forever) suit?

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 has exceeded players’ expectations since the game was released. After the disappointment of The Amazing Spider-Man 2 game, gamers were thirsting for a game that would capture the essence of Spider-Man. Insomniac Games delivered on that with Marvel’s Spider-Man for the PS4. The recently-released sequel promises to be an even better gaming experience.

Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 has much to offer players, including great gameplay, a compelling story, and a high degree of customization. In the first game, players could choose between various Spider-Man suits, some of which offered different abilities. However, Insomniac Games made suits for aesthetic purposes only in the second game.

Unlike the system in the first game, players are not forced to wear suits they don’t like in order to gain certain abilities. Players also could not use a suit they liked the look of if they needed a certain ability. Regardless, Marvel’s Spider-Man 2 offers a wide variety of suits for players to choose from, over 78 in total. You can wear the suit you like without having to worry about how it will affect your gameplay.

Pay homage to Chadwick Boseman with Black Panther Suit in Marvel’s Spider-Man 2

in Marvel’s Spider-Man 2, Miles Morales can unlock the Forever Suit. This suit highly resembles the T’Challa primary Black Panther suit, with a huge Spider emblem on the chest. The suit is perfect for adding a Wakandan flavour to your style during your adventures in New York City. The suit is also designed as a tribute to the late Chadwick Boseman, who played Black Panther in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

To get the Black Panther (Forever) Suit, you need to reach character level 26, after which the suit becomes available to unlock from your menu. Unlocking the Black Panther (Forever) suit costs 55 Tech parts and 2 rare tech parts. With these resources, you can construct and unlock the suit for Miles Morales. Additionally, there is an additional feature to suits in Marvel’s Spider-Man 2.

Many of the suits in the game come in a variety of styles. After constructing the base suit, you can unlock two or three recoloured versions of the suit. These recoloured versions will ask you for a few more of your resources. The style unlock cost for the Black Panther (Forever) suit is 25 Tech parts and 1 rare token. The Forever suit is not the only one inspired by other Marvel heroes in the game. There is also a Wolverine-inspired suit and an Arachknight suit that mixes Spider-Man and Moon Knight.
