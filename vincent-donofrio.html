Vincent D’onofrio Biography | Wikipedia

Vincent D’Onofrio is an American actor, producer and director who rose to fame for playing the role of Kingpin in Netflix’s famous drama series, Daredevil (2015 to 2018) and Hawkeye (2021) produced by the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). He also portrays the role of John Ingram in Netflix’s 2021 movie called The Unforgivable. Vincent’s other famous roles are in Ghost Wars (2018), Interrogation (2020), Ratched (2020), The Eyes Of Tammy Faye, Birds Of Paradise and more.
