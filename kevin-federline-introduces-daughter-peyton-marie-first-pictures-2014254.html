Kevin Federline Introduces Daughter Peyton Marie: First Pictures

She’s a beauty! Kevin Federline introduced his sixth child, daughter Peyton Marie, via Instagram on Thursday, April 24.

“We would like everyone to meet our newest Federline. Peyton Marie Federline. Born 4.4.14 8lbs 4oz,” the 36-year-old gushed. Alongside the caption, Britney Spears‘ ex-husband shared a sweet snapshot of the little tyke getting snuggled by big sister Jordan, 2.

“3 weeks old today,” he added, with another photo of the couple’s newborn. “Only one out of six that will have blue eyes. Didn’t think it was possible. Mommy.” In a third shot, Federline and Spears’ sons Sean, 8, and Jayden, 7, got in on the fun as they met their half-sister for the first time.
