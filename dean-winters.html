Dean Winters: Age, Family, Siblings, Girlfriend, Medical Condition, Net worth

Dean Winters is an American actor and stuntman who has been in the entertainment industry for over 25 years.

He is best known for his role as Ryan O’Reilly on the HBO prison drama Oz and his roles in the TV series Millennium, Rescue Me, 30 Rock, Sex and the City, and Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

He is also famous for portraying “Mayhem” in a series of Allstate Insurance commercials that showcase various scenarios of accidents and disasters.

But who is the man behind the mayhem?

Dean Winters age

Dean Winters was born on July 30, 1964, in New York City.

Dean Winter’s family and siblings

Dean Winters was raised on Long Island with his two brothers, Scott and Bradford, and his sister, Blair.

Dean Winter’s girlfriend

What is Dean Winter’s medical condition?

Dean Winters had a near-death experience in 2009 when he contracted a bacterial infection that caused him to go into cardiac arrest for two minutes.

He was revived by paramedics and spent three weeks in intensive care.

However, he developed gangrene as a complication and had to undergo multiple amputations of two toes and half of a thumb.

He also had several surgeries and skin grafts to recover from his ordeal.

He told Page Six that he has been living in pain ever since and suffers from neuropathy that affects his hands and feet.

Dean Winter’s net worth

He reportedly makes between $3 million and $4 million per year from the insurance company.

Conclusion

Dean Winters is a talented and versatile actor who has overcome many challenges in his life.

He is a survivor, a fighter, and a star.
